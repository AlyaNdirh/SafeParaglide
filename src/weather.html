Safety Tip

Ideal conditions for paragliding

The wind speed should be between 15 and 20 km/h. You should avoid wind gusts above 20 km/h. The rain forecast should be less than 0.8 and you should not fly in fog. If you see clouds, it is not necessarily bad.
